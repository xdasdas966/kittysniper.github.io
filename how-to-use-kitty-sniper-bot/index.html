Guide to Kitty Sniper: Efficient Trading of Solana Coins on Telegram

This guide will explore the functionalities of Kitty Sniper and provide instructions on how to utilize it effectively.

Setup

Step 1: On Telegram, search the username Kitty Sniper


Step 2: On the startup prompt, users can see their wallet address and bot settings.


Step 3: Copy the wallet address and go to your Solana wallet, you can export your private keys to import your wallet into apps like Phantom or SolFlare.


Step 4: Deposit $SOL to your Kitty Sniper wallet. Check if the $SOL has been transferred to your Kitty Sniper wallet.

Step 5: Choose the “Buy X SOL” button.


Step 6: Reply with the desired amount of token in SOL to be bought.
(Other BUY SOL buttons can be configured in your settings for preset amounts)


Step 7: Wait for the bot to confirm the transaction.

Trade Position Management on Kitty Sniper

Step 1: Type /start.


Step 2: Choose the “Manage Positions” button.


Step 3: Your trade position prompt includes the following information:

• Current Profit, both as a percentage and as an absolute.
• Current Value, in U.S. dollars and $SOL
• The Market Cap of the token you bought.
• Price Changes in the last 5 minutes, 1 hour, 6 hours, and 24 hours.

•Initial Value.
•Token Balance.
•Wallet Balance.

*Kitty Sniper is not affiliated with the token above, it is just for demonstration purposes only.

Selling Tokens on Kitty Sniper

Step 1: Type /start.
Step 2: Choose the “Manage Position” button.
Step 3: Wait for the bot to display your current position prompt.
Step 4: Choose the “Sell” button. You can choose to sell different amounts of your holdings.
Step 5: Wait for the bot to confirm the transaction.

*Kitty Sniper is not affiliated with the token above, it is just for demonstration purposes only.

Quick Tips

• Keep your workspace clean. It is ok to delete messages because messages like current positions and settings can be access quickly using /. 

• Refresh your information often to make sure you get in and out of trades fast because trading new SPL tokens can be very volatile. 

• Some settings require selecting buttons while some require manual input replies.

• In rare cases, if a transaction fails, wait a few seconds and try again. Always wait for confirmation before you redo an action.

• Always do your own research.

/start

The start menu is where you can see your wallet address and manage Kitty Sniper’s settings.

 

You can also export your private keys to your wallet, and import wallet in apps like Phantom or Solflare. 

/settings

The settings menu is where you configure settings for your Kitty Sniper bot. Here you can set quick-buy buttons depending on the minimum & maximum SOL you plan on trading.

 

You can also adjust your slippage settings and bot speed to clear transactions faster. You can also enable autobuy to have your bot automatically buy tokens without confirmation.

/Contract Search

If you want to purchase a token, simply paste the contract address into the messages.

 

Kitty Sniper will send a information prompt about the tokens you have selected. This will include the ticker, current price, block links, birdeye with option to buy.

 

With this information, you can make inform trades.

/Live P&L

After you’ve made a successful trade, this prompt will show you your trade P&L.

 

Here you can quickly look at your holdings, market cap, balance, worth and percent increases. Use this information to quicky snipe gains and exit.

 

You will also have options to buy more, sell and check tokens in your wallet. The refresh button offers second-by-second updates on your holdings.
